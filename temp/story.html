Our Story

I have measured out my life with coffee spoons. - T.S. Elliot

De Clieu is dedicated to serving high quality Arabic beans. We support local roasters who produce quality coffee while maintaining a small carbon footprint. Our beans are small batch roasted focusing on naturally processed African and Indonesian coffees. Our coffees produce intense fruit flavors with subtle chocolate notes.

We are partners with Intelligentsia Roasters !!
