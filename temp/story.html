Who is de Clieu?

The story of de Clieu's introduction of coffee comes from his account in the Année littéraire of 1774. According to this account, he arranged to transport a coffee plant (or perhaps several) from the greenhouses of the Jardin royal des plantes (which had originally received two plants from Holland in the 1710s) to Martinique in 1720.



Our Story

De Clieu is dedicated to serving high quality Arabic beans. We support local roasters who produce quality coffee while maintaining a small carbon footprint. Our beans are small batch roasted focusing on naturally processed African and Indonesian coffees. Our coffees produce intense fruit flavors with subtle chocolate notes.
